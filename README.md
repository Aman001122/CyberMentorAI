CyberMentor AI is an intelligent cybersecurity platform designed to help users recognize, understand, and respond to modern digital threats with confidence.
Users can describe suspicious incidents in simple language—such as phishing emails, scam calls, OTP fraud attempts, malicious links, social engineering attacks, or other cybersecurity concerns—and receive an instant AI-powered analysis.
The platform evaluates the threat, assigns a risk score, identifies the attack type, explains the potential danger, and provides actionable security recommendations to help users stay protected.
CyberMentor AI also maintains a personalized analysis history, allowing users to review previous investigations and track security incidents over time through an interactive dashboard.
By combining threat intelligence, risk assessment, and user-friendly guidance, CyberMentor AI acts as a virtual cybersecurity mentor that helps users make safer decisions in an increasingly complex digital world.
